About N!M

The Nim Game is a two players strategy game.


It can be played versus a computer (singleplayer mode) or versus another player (multiplayer mode).


It's played in turns and each player has to take one or more pieces from one of the piles.


The game objective is to be the last player to take the last piece of the board.


If you want to play, log in first on "Home" page!


We hope you have fun!

  • Start choosing the configuration that you want
  • After that click "Start Game" and the game will show on the side
  • To make a play, just click on a piece and all the ones on the left are removed
  • You can reread the rules by clicking in "Game Rules"
  • The "Classification" button will show how many wins the players and/or the computer have
  • After the game start, a button "Leave" will appear and you can give up the game by clicking it
  • When the game ends, a button "Play Again" will appear and you can restart the game with the same configuration by clicking it
  • Wait your turn to play
  • Each player, in turn, must take at least one piece, but they may take more than one as long as they all come from the same row.
  • It's allowed to make a row empty, effectively removing the row out of the game
  • The player taking the last piece wins
  • Have fun!
